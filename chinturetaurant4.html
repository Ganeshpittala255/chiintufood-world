chicken biryani

Hyderabadi biryani (on the left) served with other Indian dishes.
Two biryani accompaniments: mirchi ka salan and raitha/dahi chutney. Base ingredients are basmati rice, goat meat or (sometime chicken or beef), dahi, fried onion and ghee. Spices include cinnamon, cloves, cardamom (elaichi), bay leaves
